Contacting Us

If you're experiencing a problem using Serene Notes:

  1. Launch Serene Notes.
  2. Tap the menu button:
  3. Select Preferences & Help from the dropdown menu.
  4. Scroll to the bottom and tap Send E-Mail

This will create a new e-mail, addressed to us, where you can describe the problem. Configuration info will be included, helping us solve your problem.
