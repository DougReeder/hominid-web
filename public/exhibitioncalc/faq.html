How do I set Exhibition Mode to display this calculator?

  1. Launch Exhibition Preferences.
  2. Enable ExhibitionCalc. (If there's no checkbox for ExhibitionCalc, re-start your device and launch Exhibition Preferences again.)
  3. Tap the “Start Exhibition” button at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Select ExhibitionCalc from the menu in the upper left of the Exhibition display.

How do I enter negative numbers?

Use the subtraction key:
5 × - 2 =
gives you a result of -10

How do I copy the result to the clipboard?

Tap the calculator display.

How do the “M+”, “MR” and “MC” keys work?

  • M+ (memory plus) adds the current result to the value in memory.
  • MR (memory recall) replaces the current result with the value in memory.
  • MC (memory clear) clears the memory.

How do I repeat an operation?

Tapping the = key again repeats the last operation.

Can I paste from the clipboard into ExhibitionCalc?
