Zap Photoshare F.A.Q.

Your pictures on the big screen!

Why do some friends get a gallery of thumbnails, and others a list?

The thumbnail gallery only works in modern web browsers.

What gestures work in the carousel of the thumbnail gallery?

  • Flick left to go forward.
  • Flick right to go back.
  • Double-tap to zoom in or out.
  • Drag to scroll, when zoomed-in.
  • Tap outside picture to exit.

What keyboard and mouse inputs work in the carousel of the thumbnail gallery?

  • Arrow keys go forward and back.
  • Click on picture to go forward.
  • Mousewheel zooms.
  • Drag to scroll, when zoomed-in.
  • Backspace, delete, escape or tilde key exits.

How do I create and edit albums?

  • Put your phone in USB Mode. Create a new directory or add pictures to an existing directory. Don't add more than two dozen pictures, before stopping USB mode and running the Photos app (because of the webOS Media Indexer bug).
  • On the TouchPad, use the Photos & Videos app.

Why doesn't an album in the Photos (or Photos & Videos) app show up here?

  • Albums from an account such as Photobucket or Facebook can't be shared by Zap Photoshare. Copy the photos to a local album, or have your friend surf to photobucket.com or facebook.com.
  • If there aren't any pictures in the album, Zap Photoshare won't list it.
  • Otherwise, put your device in USB mode, and rename the album

If my friend's device can't connect to mine at all, what can we do?

  1. Use the Connection Menu (in the upper right) to turn your device's Wi-Fi off, then on again.
  2. Make sure you're on the same Wi-Fi network, not one on a “guest” network and the other on a “regular” network.
  3. Turn Wi-Fi on your friend's device off, then on again.

If the gallery loads slowly or partially on my friend's device, what can we do?

Change to a less-congested Wi-Fi network. :-(

How does my friend download a full-size image?

Thumbnail gallery:

  1. Click on a picture to display it in the carousel.
  2. Click the download button in the lower right.
  3. (On webOS and iOS devices) Option-tap or tap-and-hold the picture, then select Copy to Photos.
List gallery:
  1. In the lightbox, note the number in the lower-left. For example, 7 (of 15).
  2. Click outside the lightbox to dismiss it.
  3. Find the number in the left-most column, and click the full-size link in that row.
  4. (On webOS and iOS devices) Option-tap or tap-and-hold the picture, then select Copy to Photos.

In the list gallery's lightbox, the “next” button moves around when pictures have different proportions.

You can click anywhere on a picture (or use the arrow keys) to advance.

What order are my photos displayed in?

  • The “Photo Roll” album is displayed in reverse chronological order.
  • Other albums are displayed in chronological order.

Can my friend(s) contine to browse my photos, while I use other apps?

Yes! (but Zap Photoshare must be maximized to use Touch-to-Share).

How do I allow sharing to continue while my device sleeps?

In the Wi-Fi app, select Sleep Settings from the app menu, then set “When Phone Sleeps” to Keep Wi-Fi On.

Can I take advantage of Neato!, Twitter or DropBox?

Yes! From the app menu, select Copy URL Message, then paste the message into Neato!, your Twitter app, or a text file in DropBox. Your friends need to be on the same Wi-Fi network to actually view your photos.

How private is Zap Photoshare?

Anyone on the Wi-Fi network can view and download any picture in your selected album.

Does the Wi-Fi network need to be connected to the Internet?

No.
