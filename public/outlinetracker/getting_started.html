Terms

An Outline Tracker Item is a Task or Note. A Task has a checkbox
to show whether it's completed. A Note can represent a memo, a Task “on hold”, or an appointment. Either a Task or a Note can be the parent to a group of Items.
Items are grouped into Projects. Local Projects are private. Basecamp Classic Projects can be shared with others. You can put all your items in one Project, or create a new Project for any goal with more than one Task.
Task List Scenes
The first card in Outline Tracker shows Tasks to work on, from all Projects.
  • The To-Do scene shows only Tasks you can work on now.
  • To-Do & Future also shows Tasks you can't work on yet.
  • To-Do & Waiting also shows Tasks you are waiting for other people to do.
  • The Waiting scene shows Tasks you are waiting for other people to do.
  • The By Due Date scene shows all incomplete Tasks, and Notes with a Due Date, ordered by Due Date.
  • The All Items scene is used to search for items.

Outline Scenes
Each Project has a card of its own which shows an outline of all Tasks and Notes. To open an Outline scene, from the application menu, select Show Project Outline > project title.

To add a sibling after an item, select the item and tap the New Sibling button:
Children and siblings inherit details from the selected item: whether they are a Note or Task, Place, Responsible, Due Date and Lead Time.

To move an item, hold it down for half a second, then drag it up or down. If you drop it on another item, it will become a child of that item. If you drop an item between two items, it will be moved in front of the latter item. (Make sure the center of the moving item is over the center of the new parent, or over the line between two items.)
To delete an item, drag it to the left or right, just like in a list.
