(Requires webOS 2.x or 3.x)
Enabling Search & Quick Action on Smartphones (webOS 2.x)
  1. In the Preferences for Just Type, under Launch & Search, tap Add More Searches.
  2. Tap Outline Tracker.
  3. In the Launch & Search list, hold down Outline Tracker for half a second, then drag it to the top of the list.
  4. Under Quick Actions, tap Add Quick Actions.
  5. Tap New Item.
  6. In the list of Quick Actions, hold down New Item for half a second, then drag it to the top of the list.
Enabling Search & Quick Action on TouchPad (webOS 3.x)
  1. In the Preferences for Just Type, under Search Using, check the Outline Tracker box.
  2. Under Actions, check the New Item box.
Using Search
  1. Type or paste your search phrase into Just Type.
  2. Under Search Using (or Launch & Search), tap Outline Tracker.
  3. Outline Tracker will display all matching items.
  • If the Item you're looking for is not displayed, tap the backspace key to shorten your search phrase.
  • If too many items are displayed, type more characters to narrow the search.
Using the Quick Action
You can type a new item into Just Type, or copy a whole message from another application and paste it in. From Quick Actions select New Item.
Outline Tracker will split the text into summary and content text, and search the first part of the text for a due date.
If the message doesn't start with a good summary, type a summary into Just Type, select New Item, and paste the message text into the content text field.
