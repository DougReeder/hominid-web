Objective

Write the best wedding planning outline, suitable for a couple to import into Outline Tracker and customize.

Outlines should make appropriate use of the features of Outline Tracker:

  • A prerequisite for a given task should be listed as a subtask.
  • Tasks should use one of the default Places: Work, Home, Desktop Computer, Running Errands or Anywhere.

Prize

  • Fame! The winning entry will be featured on our website.
  • The warm glow of helping couples off to a smoother start in life
  • A US$20 gift card

Rules

  • The outline must import into Outline Tracker without error. [The easiest way to ensure this is to export it from Outline Tracker.]
  • One entry per person.
  • Entries must be e-mailed (preferably as an attachment) to
  • Entries must be received by 11 p.m. Eastern Standard Time Sunday, April 4, 2010.
  • Entering the contest constitutes permission to redistribute the entry
  • Employees and contractors of Hominid Software and their immediate family members are not eligible.

Judging

Two of our judges bring their experience being married last year. The other two bring their experience of planning for their wedding this year.

The judges' decision is final.
