PopupCalc Features

Full multi-tasking on the HP TouchPad!

Skip to navigation

PopupCalc is a calculator that runs in a window on top of other applications. Both PopupCalc and the other app are fully functional, so you can do calculations while writing a memo or shopping on-line.

Results are saved for later use and can be copied to the clipboard.

PopupCalc “lives” in a dashboard notification, so the calculator is just a swipe and a tap away at any time, once launched.
