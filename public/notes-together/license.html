Privacy Policy

Your data is stored on each computer you use with Notes Together. This data is accessible to anyone who uses the same account as you on that computer. If you designate a third-party server, Notes Together will also store your data there. Notes Together can read and alter your data on your designated server. Check with your provider for their privacy policy.

Notes Together does not collect nor transmit user data to its own server. Nor does it collect or transmit data to third-parties that you haven't designated.
